This map is a visualisation of the ‘non-synchronous supportable generation’ capacity information published in Powerlink’s Generation Capacity Guide. It is essential that interested parties refer to this document, which outlines the methodology and assumptions underpinning the specified capacities.

In practice, higher or lower capacities than what is indicated may be achievable. Powerlink can assist proponents to make informed investment decisions by providing more detailed and project-specific capacity and congestion information as part of a connection enquiry. Interested parties are also encouraged to refer to Powerlink’s Transmission Annual Planning Report, which provides additional information relevant to the development of new generation projects.
